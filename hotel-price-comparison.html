Compare hotel prices worldwide before you pay. Arbitrica detects your checkout and finds the lowest global rate in seconds.

Compare Before You Pay, Not After

Traditional hotel comparison tools show you different platforms side by side: Booking.com vs. Expedia vs. Hotels.com. That is useful, but it misses the biggest variable in hotel pricing: your geographic location. The same room on the same platform costs different amounts depending on which country you appear to be browsing from.

Arbitrica's pre-checkout comparison does what no other tool does. When you reach a hotel checkout page, the extension instantly searches the same room across 200 geographic regions. It does not compare platforms. It compares the same platform's price from every country on Earth.

Why Geographic Comparison Matters More Than Platform Comparison

Price differences between booking platforms for the same hotel rarely exceed 5-8%. Price differences between countries on the same platform regularly hit 20-60%. A room on Booking.com priced at $250 from the United States may show as $160 from Indonesia, $175 from Brazil, and $190 from Poland. These are real, bookable rates for the same room.

Arbitrica checks all 200 regions in parallel, so you see the full pricing landscape in under 30 seconds. The comparison appears as a simple panel showing your current price, the best price found, and the savings amount.

How the Checkout Detection Works

The Chrome extension monitors your browsing and activates when it recognizes a hotel checkout page. It reads the hotel name, dates, and room type directly from the page. No manual input is needed. The extension supports Booking.com, Expedia, Hotels.com, Agoda, Trip.com, Priceline, and all major OTAs.

Once activated, the backend fires 200 parallel price lookups. Results stream in and the extension displays the best rates as they arrive. You see the lowest price within seconds and can switch to a cheaper booking link with one click.

Real-Time Detection

The extension activates at checkout, not before. It stays invisible until you are about to pay, then shows you whether a better price exists.

No Setup Required

Install the extension and book hotels as you normally do. Arbitrica handles detection, comparison, and link generation automatically.
