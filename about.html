The problem we solve

The same hotel room costs different amounts depending on which country you book from. A room listed at $300 in the US can appear at $180 from Poland, $210 from Japan, or $195 from Brazil. This is geographic price discrimination — and it affects every major booking platform.

Until now, the only way to exploit these differences was to manually connect to VPNs one country at a time, clear cookies, switch currencies, and compare results. Most people give up after two or three countries. Arbitrica eliminates that entire process.

How we do it

200
Countries scanned

When you reach checkout, our AI runs a simultaneous price scan across 200 countries. If a lower price exists anywhere, it prepares the booking for you to review.

24/7
Post-booking monitoring

After you book, Arbitrica monitors your reservation daily. When rates fall across any of the 200 countries, it alerts you and prepares a rebooking at the better price.

$5,000
Annual savings

Frequent travellers save between $500 and $5,000 per year. The AI handles detection, global search, validation, and transaction execution — you just review and approve.

Our mission

We started with international hotel bookings because the price gaps are largest there. But geographic price arbitrage applies to far more than hotels.

Subscriptions, online courses, e-commerce, insurance, and dozens of other categories show the same pattern of location-based pricing.

Arbitrica is building the autonomous layer that finds the real lowest price for everything you buy. Our patented technology handles the entire lifecycle: detection, global search, validation, and transaction execution. People deserve the lowest price that exists. Automatically.
