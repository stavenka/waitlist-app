How the Performance Fee Works

The 10% performance fee applies only to actual savings. If Arbitrica finds a rate that is $100 cheaper than what you paid, the fee is $10. You keep $90 in savings. If Arbitrica finds no savings on a booking, you pay nothing beyond the monthly subscription.

This alignment means Arbitrica earns more only when you save more. There is no incentive to show marginal savings or waste your time with trivial price differences.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a credit card to join the waitlist?

No. The waitlist is free with no payment information required. You will only be asked for payment details if you choose the Standard plan after the early access period ends.

What happens after the first 10,000 spots fill?

New users join the Standard plan at $20/month plus 10% of savings. Early access users keep their free plan as long as their account remains active.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no contracts or cancellation fees. Cancel the Standard plan at any time and your access continues until the end of the billing cycle.
