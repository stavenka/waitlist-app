# **Gambit Technologies Privacy Policy**


**Effective Date:** December 3, 2025


This Privacy Policy describes how Gambit Technologies ("we," "us," "our," "Gambit") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information in connection with the Gambit Chrome browser extension, web application, and related services that automate deal searching and booking (collectively, "Services"). Gambit Technologies is a company registered in the United Kingdom, operating from London.


## **1. Collection of Information**


### **Sources of Collection**


We collect, process, and use information about you when you:

- Install and/or access our Services through our browser extension

- Register for our Services

- Interact with our Services (including automated deal searches and booking actions)

- Connect to our Services through third parties, including travel booking sites, airline websites, and hotel platforms

- Contact us for support

- Participate in surveys or feedback requests


We collect certain information when you submit or provide it to us directly. When you have installed our browser extension, we also automatically collect information about you and your use of the Services, as detailed below, through the use of log processes, cookies, and other tracking technologies. Certain information is collected about you from third parties, including when you connect through third-party travel services.


### **Types of Information We May Collect**


The types of information we collect about you may include personal information of the following types:


- **Identifiers** that you provide us directly, such as your first and last name, email address, and/or phone number

- **Identifiers collected through your use of the Services**, such as unique online or device identifiers, IP address, device or session IDs and information regarding your system configuration (operating system and version), network, and connected hardware (e.g., computer or mobile device) and software applications

- **Account credentials**, including username and password

- **Commercial Information** about you, such as records of travel searches, hotels, flights, car rentals, or other services you search for, view, or book through our Services, including:

  - Travel destinations, dates, and preferences

  - Hotels, airlines, or service providers you search for or view

  - Prices and deals you've been shown

  - Booking confirmations and transaction history

  - Loyalty program numbers and preferences

- **Internet and other Network Activity**, such as:

  - URLs of travel booking pages you visit while using our extension

  - Date and time stamps associated with your searches and bookings

  - Click stream data as you navigate through travel sites

  - Browser type and version

- **Payment Information** necessary to complete bookings, including payment card details (processed securely through our payment processors)

- **Location Information** (general location based on IP address; precise location only if you explicitly consent)


We do not collect or process sensitive personal information such as health data, biometric data, or precise geolocation without your explicit consent.


### **2. Use of Information We Collect**


We use information we collect about you for the following purposes:


**To Provide Our Services and Manage Your Account, including to:**

- Process your registration and maintain your account

- Enable automated deal searching and booking features

- Track your travel searches and preferences

- Process payments for bookings made through our Services

- Send you booking confirmations, updates, and administrative messages

- Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries


**To Personalize and Improve Our Services**

- Create a profile of your travel preferences to show relevant deals

- Use your search history to recommend similar destinations or services

- Analyze usage trends to improve our AI algorithms and deal-finding capabilities

- Test and optimize our booking automation features


**To Provide You Marketing Information**

- Send you personalized travel deals, promotions, and service updates via email

- Show you targeted offers based on your travel preferences

- You can opt out of marketing communications at any time


**To Protect You, Others, and our Business**

- Investigate and limit fraud, spam, and malicious activities

- Improve security measures for automated booking processes

- Assert and defend our legal rights


**To Comply With Applicable Laws**

- Comply with UK and EU data protection regulations

- Respond to lawful requests from authorities

### **4. Your Choices**


**Updating Account Information**

You may update your account information by logging into your account settings.

You can opt out of promotional emails by clicking "unsubscribe" in any email or adjusting your account preferences. You will still receive service-related communications about your bookings and account.

### **5. Your Privacy Rights (UK/EU Users)**


Under UK GDPR and EU GDPR, you have the following rights:


**Right to Access**

You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.


**Right to Rectification**

You have the right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information.


**Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")**

You have the right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions (such as ongoing booking obligations).


**Right to Restrict Processing**

You have the right to request that we limit the processing of your personal information under certain circumstances.


**Right to Data Portability**

You have the right to receive your personal information in a structured, machine-readable format.


**Right to Object**

You have the right to object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing purposes.


**Exercising Your Rights**

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@mygambit.ai or through our support portal. We will respond within 30 days as required by law.


**Right to Lodge a Complaint**

If you believe we have violated your rights, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) or your local data protection authority.
